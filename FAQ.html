14. Is my holiday protected?

Absolutely. Your contract is with British Airways Holidays whose package bookings are covered by the ATOL scheme. In the unlikely event of our insolvency, the Civil Aviation Authority will ensure that you are not stranded abroad and will arrange to refund any money you have paid us for an advance booking. For further information visit the ATOL website at www.atol.org.uk.

24. Do I need a visa to travel to New Orleans?

If you are a UK passport holder you will need what's called an ESTA (or electronic visa) for your trip and a valid ePassport. You can check requirements on https://www.cbp.gov/travel/international-visitors/visa-waiver-program.

31. What can I expect from New Orleans during Mardi Gras, is it for me?

The Mardi Gras experience and atmosphere in the city is legendary, it's definitely the wildest party of the year. The parades are vibrant and fun-packed, with nightlife that's not for the faint hearted. As you'd expect, The city is at its busiest and there'll be congestion as a result, so getting around can be challenging. We recommending visiting ba.com/NewOrleans or www.neworleanscvb.com for the best insight into all that New Orleans and Mardi Gras has to offer.

32. Will you be taking care of my plans while in New Orleans?

We'll arrange coach transfers to get you from the airport to your hotel and grandstand passes for one of the Mardi Gras parades are also included. Other than that, your time in New Orleans can be spent exactly how you choose to, there's no set itinerary - the choice is yours. As the city will be extremely busy, we recommend researching plans and making any reservations for restaurant, tours etc in advance. For inspiration, visit www.neworleanscvb.com for the best insight into all that New Orleans and Mardi Gras has to offer.

33. What time is check out from the hotel?

Usual check out is 11am. We will request a late check out for all our guests however as this is an extremely busy time in New Orleans this may not be possible. We suggest you discuss any specific requirements with the hotel when you arrive, as they may be able to guarantee a late check out for a fee (payable locally). All our chosen hotels will look after your luggage after you have checked out.
